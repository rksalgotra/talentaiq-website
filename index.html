FAQ

Common questions

How is TalentAIQ different from Greenhouse or Lever?

▼
Greenhouse and Lever are generic ATSs built for tech companies. TalentAIQ is built specifically for financial services. The differences are: (1) every AI score comes with a written explanation, (2) our skills ontology includes BFSI-specific skills like KYC, AML, Basel III, and MiFID II out of the box, (3) DEI compliance features detect algorithmic bias at the score level — not just pipeline reporting, (4) we cost 5-10x less. Greenhouse starts at ₹8L/year; TalentAIQ starts at ₹99,000/year.

Where is our candidate data stored? Does it leave India?

▼
TalentAIQ is deployed as a Docker container on your own cloud infrastructure — AWS Mumbai, Azure India, or any cloud you choose. Your candidate data never leaves your environment. There is no shared SaaS database. This directly addresses RBI data localisation requirements for NBFCs and regulated entities.

How long does implementation take?

▼
A typical implementation takes 1-3 days. Day 1: Docker deployment and database setup. Day 2: Careers site configured with your branding and domain. Day 3: Import existing candidates, configure workflows, and publish first job. You can start receiving applications on LinkedIn the same week you sign up.

How does the AI scoring work? Is it really explainable?

▼
Every candidate-job match has three components: semantic similarity (50%) — how closely the resume text matches the job description using pgvector embeddings; skills score (35%) — overlap between candidate's skills and job's required skills using our BFSI ontology; and experience score (15%) — years of experience vs job requirement. Each score comes with a written explanation generated by Claude that a non-technical recruiter can understand and defend to their board.

Do you offer a free trial?

▼
Yes. All plans include a 90-day free pilot with full access to every feature. No credit card required. We want you to see real results — hires made, DEI reports generated, time-to-fill reduced — before you commit to an annual plan. If you don't see value in 90 days, you owe us nothing.

Can we migrate data from our current ATS?

▼
Yes. TalentAIQ supports CSV import for candidates and applications. We also have an ATS integration layer that connects to Greenhouse, Lever, and other major ATSs via their APIs. Your historical candidate data, job postings, and application history can be migrated as part of the onboarding process.
